## Unit Description

This course is designed to present an overview and discuss major topics of regional diplomacy of the People's Republic of China (PRC).

Beginning with a brief introduction of a historical review of China's role in the region, the main body of the course is divided into the following sections:

1. The development of Chinese regional diplomacy after the founding of the PRC
2. China's foreign policy toward different types of neighbours
3. Domestic determinants and their impact on regional diplomacy
4. Current issues and the future of China's regional policy and its implications on China's role in the world
