### Part 3 — Integration triggered governance and sovereignty responses
- 关键词：AI Basic Act, high-impact AI, transparency, local representative, sovereign AI foundation model
- 作用：说明韩国如何把 AI 从市场问题升级为国家治理与战略自主问题

## 一句话结论模板

The most important impact of U.S.-developed LLMs in South Korea has been to transform AI from an efficiency-enhancing technology into a question of strategic dependence and state capacity.
