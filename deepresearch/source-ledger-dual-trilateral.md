## Claim map

- C1: Northeast Asia is best described, as of March 26, 2026, as moving toward an uneven "dual-trilateral" structure rather than a simple binary bloc order.
  - Supporting sources: L1, L2, L4, L5, W1, W2, W3, W4, W5, W7, W8, W9, W10, S1, S2, S3, S4
  - direct_or_inferred: inferred from combined institutional evidence
  - confidence: high

- C2: The China-Japan-ROK track has revived as a regularized but security-light mechanism centered on low-politics, economic, social, and crisis-management functions.
  - Supporting sources: L5, L6, L8, W1, W2, S1, S3, S5
  - direct_or_inferred: direct plus inferred
  - confidence: high

- C3: The U.S.-Japan-ROK track has become much more institutionalized after August 18, 2023, especially in consultation, exercises, missile warning, and extended deterrence mechanisms.
  - Supporting sources: L4, W3, W4, W5, W7, W8, W9, W10, S2, S4
  - direct_or_inferred: direct
  - confidence: high

- C4: The coexistence of the two trilaterals reflects issue bifurcation in regional order: security is increasingly organized through U.S.-anchored minilateral deterrence, while economic and functional cooperation with China remains necessary and partially institutionalized.
  - Supporting sources: L2, L4, L5, L6, L7, W1, W2, W3, W4, W6, S1, S3, S4, S5
  - direct_or_inferred: inferred
  - confidence: medium-high

- C5: For China, the CJK track is strategically valuable not because it replaces U.S.-Japan-ROK deterrence, but because it prevents complete bloc consolidation and preserves room for influence over Tokyo and Seoul.
  - Supporting sources: L2, L3, W1, W2, W6, S1, S5
  - direct_or_inferred: inferred
  - confidence: medium-high

- C6: For Japan and South Korea, the dual-trilateral structure is a hedging device: hard security is outsourced to the U.S.-alliance network, while CJK diplomacy manages economic dependence, communication channels, and the risk of full strategic rupture with China.
  - Supporting sources: L2, L5, W1, W2, W3, W5, W8, W10, S2, S3, S4, S5
  - direct_or_inferred: inferred
  - confidence: medium-high

- C7: The structure remains incomplete and politically reversible because CJK implementation is weak and U.S.-Japan-ROK cooperation still depends on domestic political continuity in Washington, Tokyo, and Seoul.
  - Supporting sources: L7, W2, S1, S2, S4
  - direct_or_inferred: inferred
  - confidence: medium-high
