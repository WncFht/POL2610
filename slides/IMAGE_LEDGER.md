## Notes

- Prefer official or Commons-hosted photos first, then DVIDS, then self-generated diagrams.
- For MOFA-hosted PDFs, browser access was reliable but direct shell download was unstable on this machine through the current proxy path.
- If more visuals are needed, the next easiest additions are:
  - screenshot-like cards from official statement pages
  - cropped variants of the existing summit/security photos
  - additional local PDF first-page renders from `mineru`-related course readings
  - theory-page screenshots derived from `slides/research/`
